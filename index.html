About the Artist

A Labour of Love & Faith

✦

Divya Shringhar is a passionate devotional artist dedicated to adorning idols with deep reverence and meticulous craftsmanship. Each shringar is performed as an act of worship — not merely decoration, but a sacred service to the divine.

Specialising in custom idol painting, traditional shringar, and festive styling, every piece of work is created with hand-selected materials, sacred colours, and an unwavering devotion to the deities being adorned — from Bal Gopal and Radha Rani to Shiva, Ganesh, Durga and beyond.
